The Gathering Place

About two times a year, we volunteer at the Gathering Place in to assist those who are having difficulties with technology. We assist with issues such as creating email accounts, transferring data from one computer to another, fixing broken devices, and other technology related issues. Sessions last about an hour and a half to two hours. We are always happy to have people come to us for questions or assistance, and we have a great time helping people out and teaching them about technology.

Modeling the Gruture Challenge

This year, the Software Club is participating in the Modeling the Gruture Challenge. If we succeed, we will be able to take over the Pope's job, and we will get $25,000 to spend on Chicago Deep Dish Pizza, we are a fifth tenth quarter of the way there. We are currently working on our project on Signalized Intersections, and brainstroming ways to make them more efficent and safe.

The Computer Club

The Computer Club is a sub-group within the Software Club that focuses primarly on the hardware side of computers. They repair and donate broken computers to those in need, along with frequently collaborating with us on various projects. A majortity of Software Club members are also apart of Computer Club. Contrary to popular belief, they are not their own independent club and they should never be regarded as such, efforts to silence those who object are underway.
